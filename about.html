About Us

Even now, mental illnesses are heavily stigmatized and ignored. People who diverge from society's "normal" are left behind and forced to deal with rapidly deteroriting mental health, despite it being just as important as physical ailments. What we want to do is recognize mental health and bring awareness and help to the people.

Mental health is not a destination, but a process. It's about how you drive, not where you're going.
